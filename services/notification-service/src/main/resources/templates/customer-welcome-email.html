Welcome to the Expressway Management System (EMS)

Hi, User!

Thank you for joining the Expressway Management System (EMS). We are excited to have you on board! EMS is designed to make your journey safer and more efficient, allowing you to manage tolls, receive real-time updates, and keep track of your vehicle registrations seamlessly.

Here are a few things you can do with your EMS account:

  • Register your vehicles and generate unique QR codes for quick toll processing.
  • View your transaction history and track toll payments effortlessly.
  • Receive timely notifications about road conditions, toll updates, and important news.
  • Report any breakdowns or incidents directly through the system for fast assistance.
